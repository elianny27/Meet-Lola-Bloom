Lola Bloom

Lola Bloom brings stories to life through bold, expressive animation. With a flair for color, movement, and emotion, her work captures attention and sparks imagination. Whether it's a whimsical short, a character-driven scene, or a looping visual for the web, Lola’s animations reflect her creative spirit and New York edge. Every frame is carefully crafted to tell a story — and every story is unmistakably Lola.
